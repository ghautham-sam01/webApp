"A journey of a thousand miles begins with a single step"

-Lao Tzu

I strictly adhere to this quote as if you want to master anything you must start at the beginning of it. There are no shortcuts. Though you might fail more times than you can count the numbers of star in the sky, you must always keep forward and learn from your mistakes. With every mistake you able to fine tune yourself and be that much closer to your goals and achievements. If live has taught has taught me anything, it is that hardships are inevitable but not permanent so keep moving forward. It really is mind over matter and the mental fortitude that you have will endure through that darkest times. I can overcome anything as long as I can put my mind to it.
